# ⚫ Synthetic Rubber (T2)

> [!INFO] Tier 2 rubber
> A proper elastomer polymerized from butadiene. Springy and consistent - the
> mid-game standard for belts, seals, and tires-to-be.

---

## Main recipe - Butadiene Polymerization

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    BUTA[Butadiene: 30] --> M{{Polymerizer}} --> SR[Synthetic Rubber: 30]
```

|          | Input        | Output              | Building    | Time |
| -------- | ------------ | ------------------- | ----------- | ---- |
| **Main** | 30 Butadiene | 20 Synthetic Rubber | Polymerizer | 4 s  |

---

## Alternate 1 - Integrated Gas Route

Crack and polymerize straight from refinery gas.

| Input                      | Output              | Building    | Time |
| -------------------------- | ------------------- | ----------- | ---- |
| 50 Refinery Gas + 10 Water | 10 Synthetic Rubber | Polymerizer | 6 s  |

## Alternate 2 - Crude Upgrading

Re-polymerize T1 crude rubber into a more efficient rubber.

| Input                          | Output              | Building    | Time |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------- | ----------- | ---- |
| 30 Crude Rubber + 10 Butadiene | 30 Synthetic Rubber | Polymerizer | 6 s  |
