# ⚫ Vulcanized Rubber (T3)

> [!INFO] Tier 3 rubber
> Synthetic rubber cured under heat and pressure - heat-resistant, durable, and
> springy under load. Surplus sulphur can optionally boost the cure.

---

## Main recipe - Heat Vulcanization

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    SR[Synthetic Rubber: 30] --> M{{Curing Oven}}
    M --> VR[Vulcanized Rubber: 30]
```

| | Input | Output | Building | Time |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Main** | 30 Synthetic Rubber | 30 Vulcanized Rubber | Curing Oven | 6 s |

---

## Alternate 1 - Sulfur Cure *(optional bonus)*

Spend surplus sulfur for a higher-yield, higher-grade cure - never required.

| Input | Output | Building | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| 30 Synthetic Rubber + 10 Sulfur | 40 Vulcanized Rubber | Curing Oven | 6 s |

---

## Alternate 2 - Crude Shortcut

Vulcanize cheap crude rubber directly - lower grade, but no synthetic needed.

| Input | Output | Building | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| 30 Crude Rubber | 25 Vulcanized Rubber | Curing Oven | 8 s |
